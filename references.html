Project References

Taylor's Theorem Learning Platform - Research Sources

This project was developed based on comprehensive research from authoritative textbooks, online educational resources, and additional academic readings. Below are the primary references used in developing the Taylor's Theorem Interactive Learning Platform.

Textbooks
Primary Academic References
  • 1
    Thomas' Calculus
    Thomas, G. B., et al.
    Pearson Education
    14th Edition

    Comprehensive coverage of calculus concepts including detailed explanations of Taylor series and polynomial approximations.

  • 2
    Calculus: Early Transcendentals
    Stewart, J.
    Cengage Learning
    8th Edition

    Provides clear explanations of Taylor and Maclaurin series with practical applications in engineering.

  • 3
    Advanced Engineering Mathematics
    Kreyszig, E.
    Wiley
    10th Edition

    Advanced mathematical methods with emphasis on engineering applications of Taylor series expansions.

  • 4
    Numerical Methods for Engineers
    Chapra, S. C., & Canale, R. P.
    McGraw-Hill
    7th Edition

    Practical implementation of Taylor series in numerical methods and computational applications.
